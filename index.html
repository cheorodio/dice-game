goal

The first player to reach a global score of 100 wins!

How to play

  1. In each turn, a player rolls the die as many times as they wish. Each result will get accumulated to their 'CURRENT SCORE'.
  2. But if the player rolls a 1, all of the current score will get deleted. Then it's the next player's turn.
  3. The player can choose to hold their current score by clicking the 'HOLD' button. The current score will then get transfered to the global score. Then it's the next player's turn
  4. The first player to reach 100 points in the global score wins the game
